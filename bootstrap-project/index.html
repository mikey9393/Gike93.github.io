Benefits

Lion’s mane mushrooms, also known as hou tou gu or yamabushitake, are large, white, shaggy mushrooms that resemble a lion’s mane as they grow. Reported in many studies, Lion's Mane has been shown to stimulate the synthesis of nerve growth factor (NGF), a protein that supports the growth and normal function of nerve cells.

Chaga stands out as a free radical-fighting powerhouse. Nutritionally dense, it contains beta glucans, amino acids, potassium, zinc, calcium and vitamins such as biotin, vitamin D, and B-vitamins, as well as other health-supporting nutrients.

The reishi mushroom, also known as Ganoderma lucidum and lingzhi, is a fungus that grows in various hot and humid locations in Asia. Reishi contains powerful, natural beta glucans that support immune system functionality. Considered by many to be a superior adaptogen, Reishi works with your body to help manage everyday physical, mental and emotional stressors and restore balance in a natural and healthy way.
